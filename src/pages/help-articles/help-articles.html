Set or delete alarms

You can make and change alarms in the Clock app.


Set an alarm time

  1. Open Clock app.
  2. At the top, tap Alarm.
  3. To add an alarm, tap Add .
  4. Set the alarm time.
    1. Tap the hour.
    2. Tap the minutes.
  5. Tap OK.

Turn alarm on or off

  1. Open Clock app.
  2. At the top, tap Alarm.
  3. On the alarm you want, tap the On/Off switch.

Delete an alarm

  1. Open Clock app.
  2. At the top, tap Alarm.
  3. On the alarm you want, tap Delete.

Use the timer & stopwatch

You can time yourself with the Clock app's timer or stopwatch.


Timer

You can count time down to zero with the timer.

Use the timer

  1. Open Clock app.
  2. At the top, tap Timer.
  3. Enter how long you want the timer to run.
  4. Tap Start .
  5. When your timer finishes, you'll notice vibrating. To stop this, tap Stop.

Change a running timer

  • To pause the timer, tap Pause .
  • To add a minute to the length, tap +1:00.
  • To remove the timer, tap Delete.

Stopwatch

You can count time up from zero with the stopwatch.

Use the stopwatch

  1. Open Clock app.
  2. At the top, tap Stopwatch.
  3. Tap Start .

Change a running stopwatch

  • To pause the stopwatch, tap Pause .
  • To reset the stopwatch, tap Pause Reset.
